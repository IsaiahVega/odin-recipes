Classic Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza recipe brings together a crispy, golden crust, rich tomato sauce, melty mozzarella, and your favorite toppings-all baked to perfection. Whether you're a fan of pepperoni, veggies, or just plain cheese, this recipe offers a delicious and customizable base that's perfect for any pizza night. With simple ingredients and easy-to-follow steps, you can enjoy the taste of pizzeria-quality pizza right from your own kitchen.

List of Ingredients

    For the dough:

  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 F or 45 C)
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

    • For the sauce:

  • 1 cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For the toppings(customizable)

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices(optional)
  • Sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Step-By-Step Process

  1. Prepare the dough:

    • In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
    • Stir in olive oil, salt, and flour gradually until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dought in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a cloth, and let rise for 1-1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.

  2. Make the sauce:

    • In a small saucepan, heat olive oil and saut√© the garlic for 1 minute.
    • Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, then let cool.

  3. Preheat the oven:

    • preheat your oven to 475 F (245 C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven now heat up.

  4. Shape the dough:

    • Punch down the risen dough and divide it in half (for two pizzas).
    • Roll out one portion on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

  5. Assemble the pizza:

    • Transfer the dought to a baking sheet or pizza peel.
    • Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
    • Add shredded mozzarella and you favorite toppings

  6. Bake:

    • Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  7. Serve:

    • Let cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
