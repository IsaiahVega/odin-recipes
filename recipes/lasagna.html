Lasagna Recipe

Description

This traditional lasagna recipe layers rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets for a comforting, hearty mea that's perfect for family dinners. Packed with flavor and cheesy goodness, it's a dish that never fails to impress. Whether you're making it for a weeknight treat or a special occasion, this recipe delivers classic Italian taste in every bite.

Ingredients

    For the meat sauce:

  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For th béchamel (white sauce):

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 5 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt to taste

    • Other:

  • 9 lasagna noodles (or enough for 3 layers)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil (for cooking and noodles)

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Cook the noodles

    • Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water according to package instructions.
    • Drain, toss with a bit of olive oil to prevent sticking, and set aside.

  2. Prepare the meat sauce:

    • In a large skillet, cook the onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
    • Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer on low heat for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Make the béchamel sauce

    • In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    • Whisk in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
    • Gradually whisk in milk until smooth.
    • Simmer until thickened, then add a pinch of nutmeg and salt.

  4. Preheat the oven:

    • Set your oven to 375 F (190 C).

  5. Assemble the lasagna

    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    • Add a layer of noodles, then more meat sauce, then béchamel, then cheese
    • Repeat layers until ingredients ae used, finishing with cheese on top.

  6. Bake:

    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake another 15-20 minutes, until bubbly and golden on top

  7. Rest and serve:

    • Let lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
    • Serve hot and enjoy
